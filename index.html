About

Hello, and welcome to my website.
I am a student at University of Nevada Las Vegas studying computer science, where I am actively pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree. The world of technology continues to grow at an exponential rate, while the demand for software solutions continue to rise proportionately.

Actively tutoring at College of Southern Nevada Centers for Academic Success mentoring CS students, allowing them to excel in their studies and achieve a better understanding of programming languages, concepts, and algorithms.

Currently involved in the Drumbots, a world-renowned international LED percussion act at the heart of Las Vegas since 2017, well known as the official NHL drumline for 2023 Stanley Cup Champions, the Vegas Golden Knights.
