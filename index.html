Hello, and welcome.
I am a student at University of Nevada Las Vegas studying computer science, where I am actively pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree.

Actively tutoring at College of Southern Nevada Centers for Academic Success mentoring CS students, allowing them to excel in their studies and achieve a better understanding of programming languages, concepts, and algorithms.

Currently involved in the Drumbots, a world-renowned international LED percussion act at the heart of Las Vegas since their beginning in 2016. They are well known as the Knightline, the official NHL drumline for 2023 Stanley Cup Champions, the Vegas Golden Knights. The Drumbots of Las Vegas also perform for several large-scale corporate clients for entertainment, such as Google, Microsoft, and many other companies both large and small.

The world of technology continues to grow at an exponential rate, while the demand for software solutions, development, and engineering continue to rise proportionately. My unique experiences in the world of entertainment help influence a world that bridges creativity and innovation, fueling a desire for software solutions that meet current challenges and anticipate the needs of tomorrow's digital landscape.
I believe that the future of software thrives in a world that merges creativity and technology.
